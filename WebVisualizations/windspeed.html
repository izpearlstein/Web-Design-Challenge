Wind Speed

There might be a slight correlation between latitude and wind speed, as the higher latitudes appear to have higher windspeeds. On average, it could be due to more mountain ranges in the northern hemisphere, which garner higher wind speeds at higher altitudes. This would require further analysis, however.
